<idea>
  <design_movement>Seasonal Zen Minimalism</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Seasonal Immersion**: The design adapts to the current featured season (Cherry Blossoms) with immersive full-width visuals, creating an emotional connection immediately.
    2. **Clarity & Focus**: High contrast between content and whitespace. The interface prioritizes the search function and visual discovery of destinations without clutter.
    3. **Trust & Tradition**: Using a layout that feels established and reliable, mirroring the structure of reputable travel agencies but with a modern, cleaner execution.
    4. **Direct Action**: Clear, bold calls-to-action (CTAs) that guide the user effortlessly from inspiration to booking.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **Sakura Red & Slate Black**: 
    - **Primary**: #D32F2F (Deep Red) - Used for primary buttons and accents, evoking energy, luck (in Asian culture), and urgency. Matches the "Peony Tours" reference vibe but adapted for PACK&GO.
    - **Secondary**: #F8F9FA (Off-White) - Backgrounds to keep the content readable and airy.
    - **Text**: #1A1A1A (Soft Black) - For high readability without the harshness of pure black.
    - **Accent**: #FFCDD2 (Pale Pink) - Subtle highlights and hover states, reinforcing the cherry blossom theme.
    *Reasoning*: This palette balances the excitement of travel (Red) with the reliability of a professional service (Black/White), directly referencing the provided screenshot's aesthetic.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **The "Horizon" Stack**:
    - **Hero**: Full-width immersive background with a floating, centralized search console that bridges the hero and the content below.
    - **Grid**: A strict, masonry-like grid for destinations that feels like a photo album or postcard collection.
    - **Cards**: "Best Seller" items use a classic card layout with image top, content bottom, and a distinct "badge" for offers.
    - **Footer**: A solid, grounded dark footer that anchors the page.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Floating Search Console**: A semi-transparent dark bar overlaying the hero image, acting as the command center.
    2. **Polaroid-style Destination Cards**: Images with subtle shadows and clear, overlaid typography that invites clicking.
    3. **Seasonal Motifs**: Subtle petal textures or soft gradients in section dividers to maintain the theme.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Discover & Drift"**:
    - Hovering over destination cards causes a gentle zoom (drift) effect, simulating the feeling of moving closer to a destination.
    - The search bar feels tactile, with distinct active states for inputs.
    - Scrolling is smooth, with sections fading in gently to maintain a relaxed browsing pace.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Hero Load**: Title fades in with a slight upward drift.
    - **Hover**: Destination images scale up (1.05x) smoothly (0.4s ease-out).
    - **Buttons**: Ripple effect on click; slight lift and shadow increase on hover.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings**: *Noto Serif TC* (or similar serif) for a touch of elegance and tradition, used in section titles ("熱門目的地", "精選旅程").
    - **Body**: *Noto Sans TC* (sans-serif) for maximum readability in UI elements, prices, and descriptions.
    - **Hierarchy**: Large, bold serif headings > Medium sans-serif labels > Small, high-contrast meta text.
  </typography_system>
</idea>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Monochrome Luxury</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Premium Exclusivity**: A stark, high-contrast black and white aesthetic that positions PACK&GO as a high-end, boutique travel concierge.
    2. **Visual Storytelling**: Letting the destination photos provide the *only* color on the page, making them pop dramatically against the monochrome UI.
    3. **Sophisticated Typography**: Using type as a primary design element, with oversized headings and editorial layouts.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **Onyx & Alabaster**:
    - **Primary**: #000000 (Pure Black) - For the logo, navigation, and primary buttons.
    - **Background**: #FFFFFF (Pure White) - For a gallery-like feel.
    - **Text**: #333333 (Charcoal) - For body text.
    - **No Accents**: No colored buttons or borders. All interactive elements rely on shape, size, and contrast.
    *Reasoning*: This aligns perfectly with the user's provided "PACK&GO" logo (black bag/plane) and business card, creating a cohesive brand identity that feels more upscale than the reference.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **Editorial Asymmetry**:
    - **Hero**: Split screen or heavy typographic overlay.
    - **Destinations**: A broken grid layout where images vary in size and aspect ratio, creating a dynamic, magazine-like rhythm.
    - **Whitespace**: Massive margins and padding to create a sense of luxury and calm.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Monogram Watermarks**: Subtle use of the "P&G" or plane icon as a background watermark.
    2. **Line Art Icons**: Thin, refined icons for services (flight, hotel, etc.) matching the business card style.
    3. **High-Contrast Buttons**: Solid black buttons with white text, rectangular and sharp.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Elegant Precision"**:
    - Interactions are snappy and precise.
    - Hover effects are minimal—perhaps a simple underline or a shift to negative (black turns white).
    - Focus on "white glove" feeling—smooth, frictionless forms.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Parallax**: Subtle parallax scrolling on large destination images.
    - **Reveal**: Text reveals line-by-line from behind masks.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Headings**: *Playfair Display* - High-contrast serif for a fashion-editorial look.
    - **Body**: *Montserrat* - Geometric sans-serif that complements the sharp lines of the logo.
  </typography_system>
</idea>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Modern Explorer</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Utility & Discovery**: A tool-first approach where the search and filter capabilities are front and center, styled like a modern travel app (Airbnb/Booking style).
    2. **Vibrant & Friendly**: Using a broader color palette derived from nature (sky blues, sunset oranges) to make travel feel accessible and fun.
    3. **Card-Based Interface**: Everything is a card—modular, stackable, and mobile-friendly.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **Global Vivid**:
    - **Primary**: #0066CC (Ocean Blue) - Trustworthy and travel-related.
    - **Secondary**: #FF9900 (Sunset Orange) - For call-to-actions and "Best Seller" tags.
    - **Background**: #F0F2F5 (Light Gray) - To separate cards from the background.
    *Reasoning*: A departure from the reference's specific red, aiming for a more "tech-company" travel vibe.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **The "Dashboard"**:
    - **Hero**: Functional search bar is the hero, with a background slideshow.
    - **Content**: Horizontal scrolling lists (carousels) for destinations and deals to maximize information density.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Rounded UI**: Heavy use of rounded corners (16px+) on cards and buttons.
    2. **Shadows**: Soft, diffuse shadows to create depth (Material Design influence).
    3. **Iconography**: Colorful, flat illustrations for categories.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"App-like Fluidity"**:
    - Swiping gestures on mobile.
    - Instant feedback on filter clicks.
    - Modals for quick views of itineraries.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    - **Slide**: Carousels slide smoothly.
    - **Pop**: Buttons scale down slightly on press.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - **Font**: *Inter* or *Roboto* - Purely functional, UI-focused sans-serif.
  </typography_system>
